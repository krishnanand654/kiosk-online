About Us

The KIOSK online is an online booking system which helps the students and faculties to book their refreshments according to the items provided by the KIOSK Manager. The details of the items are updated according to the stocks in KIOSK by the KIOSK Manager. The students or faculties can book refreshments before going to KIOSK and items will be ready by the time them get there. This will help to reduce the crowd and fast delivery of items and helps students to reach back to class at time
